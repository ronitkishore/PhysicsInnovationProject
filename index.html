Button Controlled Traffic Light System

The project implements a traffic light system that can be controlled by a pedestrian using a button. IF the traffic is flowing and a pedestrian standing on the side wants to cross the road, then he/she can press the button and after some time the lights turn red, and stay red for some time while the pedestrian crosses the road and then goes back to green.
